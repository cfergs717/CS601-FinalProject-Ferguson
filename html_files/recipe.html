The following recipe is a (very) basic example:

A New England IPA
Ingredient Time (when to add) Amount
Water Mash In Approx. 350 gals.
Calcium Chloride Mash In 2 lbs.
Lactic Acid Mash In 200 mls
Pilsen Malt Mash In 800 lbs.
Flaked Oats Mash In 250 lbs.
Carapils Malt Mash In 27.5 lbs.
Magnum Hops 0 mins into boil 2.5 lbs.
Citra Hops 85 mins into boil 5.5 lbs.
Mosaic Hops Whirlpool 11 lbs.
Citra Hops Whirlpool 22 lbs.
Yeast Knockout Depends on strain!
Ferment at 70 F for 5-7 days. Dry hopping, filtration, carbonation and packaging steps not included.

*This is a commercial scale recipe for a 10 bbl (barrel) brewhouse. 1 bbl = 31 gallons.
